⚡ Grammar
3rd person — the verb gets an -s! Let's see how it works.
📊
She / He + verb + s
I build → She builds · I grow → He grows
⚡ The rule
With I → the verb stays the same
With she / he → add -s or -es
Special: have → has go → goes do → does

Frida Kahlo — The Painter
A real person with an amazing job!
Frida Kahlo is a famous painter from Mexico. She paints beautiful pictures. She uses lots of colours — red, blue, yellow and orange.

Frida works in her studio. She loves flowers and animals. She wears colourful clothes and flowers in her hair.

Frida doesn't build houses. She doesn't drive a taxi. She paints her life, her feelings and her world.

She is an artist. Artists create beautiful things!
